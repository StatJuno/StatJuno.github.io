About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), advised by Prof. Kijung Shin.

Previously, I received my M.S. in Statistics and Data Science and B.A. in Applied Statistics from Yonsei University, where I was advised by Prof. Seyoung Park.

My primary research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and statistics, especially in graph-based applications.
